Connacht/Connachta

Connacht (Irish: Connachta), formerly spelled Connaught, is one of the provinces of Ireland, in the west of the country. Conncacht is the least inhabited Province in Ireland with a population of just over 400,000. Connacht is also the Province with the most Irish speaking people. The language is kept alive in communities where it is spoken today. These areas are collectively known as the Gaeltacht. The primary business centre of Connaught, and most densely populated area, is the thriving city of Galway to the south of the province. Connaught has some of the most beautiful and unspoilt countryside to be found in Ireland, including the spectacular mountainous landscape of Connemara.
